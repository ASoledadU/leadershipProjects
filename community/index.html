Who are We?

We are a group of potential full-stack web developers. Giving kids the opportunity to get a head start on using the most efficient and relevant technologies for the future. Our goal as a team is to show compassion integrity and also keep good energy flowing with the kids. We want to mold the youth into doing something that will only benefit them for the better and in our defense we believe coding is a very positive and productive way to keep kids moving in the right path.
